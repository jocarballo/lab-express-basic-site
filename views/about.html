Who is Bo Burnham...

Robert Pickering "Bo" Burnham (born August 21, 1990) is an American stand-up comedian, actor, singer, musician, and filmmaker. He began his career on YouTube in 2006, with his videos gaining over 529 million views as of November 2021. (Font: Wikipedia)

He is currently nominated for Grammy Awards for: "Best Music Film" and "Best Song Written for Visual Media".
